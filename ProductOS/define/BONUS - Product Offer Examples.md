| Component | Decision |
| --- | --- |
| Customer | Engineering and product teams at fast-growing software startups (10–500 people) who feel slowed down by Jira's configuration sprawl but need more workflow rigor than Trello or Notion can provide. |
| Outcome | One always-current source of truth across PMs, designers, and engineers — issues, projects, and cycles updated in real time so the team can plan, ship, and review without status meetings or "is this ticket still right?" Slack pings. |

| Guarantee | Free for unlimited members on the Standard plan with unlimited issues, projects, and current-cycle scope — no credit card, no time-limited trial. Cancel paid plans in one click and keep your read-only data. |
| Proof | Used by Vercel, Ramp, Mercury, Cash App, Loom, Scale AI, and \~10,000 other software companies. 4.7/5 on G2 across 1,000+ reviews citing "fastest tool I've used"; published case studies show average team adoption in under a week. |

## Example 4: Designjoy (Productized Design Service)

Unlimited senior design work for one flat monthly fee, run by a single designer with no proposals or contracts.

| Component | Decision |
| --- | --- |
| Customer | Bootstrapped SaaS founders, indie hackers, and Series A startups who need consistent senior-level design — landing pages, app UI, brand assets — but can't justify a full-time designer hire or a six-month agency retainer. |
| Outcome | A polished design deliverable every business day, sent in Figma, across landing pages, app screens, brand systems, and decks — with unlimited revisions and as many brand projects as you need under one flat monthly subscription. |
| Mechanism | One senior designer (Brett) handles every account, working a strict one-request-at-a-time queue. The constraint forces tight scope from the client, keeps quality consistent across deliverables, and eliminates the proposals, scoping calls, and SOW friction that slow agencies. |
| Guarantee | Pause or cancel anytime with no contract and no notice period — you keep every file already delivered. Unhappy with a deliverable? Unlimited revisions until it lands. |
| Proof | $1.5M+ ARR run solo, with monthly Stripe MRR screenshots posted publicly on X to 100K+ followers. Client work and testimonials from Castmagic, Tally, OpenPhone, and dozens of Product Hunt #1 launches; routinely cited as the canonical productized-service case study. |

## Example 5: Splice (Two-Sided Marketplace)

A royalty-cleared sample marketplace with a credit-based subscription layered on top of direct producer sales.

| Component | Decision |
| --- | --- |
| Customer | Bedroom and professional music producers working in Ableton, FL Studio, or Logic on electronic, hip-hop, and pop tracks — who need a steady stream of fresh, legally-cleared samples without combing pirate forums or paying $40–$60 per loop pack. |
| Outcome | One-click access to a 2M+ sample library — drum loops, melodic loops, vocal one-shots, MIDI, presets — every sample royalty-cleared for commercial release on Spotify, YouTube, or any DSP, the moment it's downloaded. |
| Mechanism | A credit-based subscription stacked over a curated producer marketplace, with labels and named artists (KSHMR, Boys Noize, Monstercat) licensing packs per-download. AI-assisted Similar Sounds, Stacks, and key/BPM filtering surface usable loops in seconds — replacing hours of YouTube-rip browsing. |
| Guarantee | Cancel anytime and keep every sample you've ever downloaded — your library stays royalty-cleared for use in your tracks even after the subscription lapses. |
| Proof | 4M+ registered producers; samples credited on Grammy-winning records (Billie Eilish, Lizzo, The Weeknd collaborators); native integration with Ableton via Splice Bridge; recommended by producer YouTube channels from Andrew Huang to In The Mix. |
