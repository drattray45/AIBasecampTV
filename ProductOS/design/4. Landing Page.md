# Landing Page

*Drafted: June 2026. Generated from `docs/PRODUCT.md`, `design/1. Product Identity.md`, and `design/2. Magic Moment.md`.*

## Summary

A **Try-Before-Signup hero** (Lovable's "value before the gate" pattern, adapted for a warm consumer lead magnet): the page shows a real AI answer she can read, then offers the free Starter Kit. 11 sections, beacon-mode hero into warm-paper body. The hero promise *is* the magic moment — "your first real AI win" — and there is **one** CTA repeated throughout: **"Get my free Starter Kit."**

> **Honesty note (pre-launch):** there are no customer logos, testimonials, or star ratings yet, and this brand's whole credibility rests on *not faking it.* The Social Proof and Deep Social Proof sections are written as **honestly reserved** — real beginner before/afters drop in only once founding members share them (earned-proof-only, per the Visual World rule). Interim trust comes from what's true today: it's free, it's jargon-free, and the founder spent three years making the mistakes so she doesn't have to.

## The Magic Moment we're promising

> Ask AI about something you already know, get an answer that's obviously good, and feel "I did that — and I can tell it's right."

**Hero promise:** "Feel behind on AI? You're not — here's your first step."
**Primary CTA:** "Get my free Starter Kit"

## Anti-patterns avoided

- **Stock-illustration hero** — replaced with Scout + a real, readable worked example (the brand's "no stock people, ever" rule).
- **Multiple CTAs above the fold** — one CTA only, repeated identically (hero, sticky, final): "Get my free Starter Kit."
- **Fabricated social proof** — logos/testimonials/ratings are honestly reserved until real; faking them would break the self-binding trust the whole brand rests on.
- **Hidden pricing** — free is front-and-center; the paid ladder is shown plainly, no recurring charge, no FOMO.
- **"Submit"-style cold CTAs / fear copy** — first-person, warm CTA; no "don't get left behind," no countdowns (kill-list enforced).

## Refresh cadence

Recommended next refresh: **45 days** from launch. **First A/B test:** the hero headline — the "you're not behind" reframe vs. an outcome-led variant ("Do 5 real AI tasks this week"). **Refresh trigger:** landing → Starter-Kit-signup conversion below target (set the number once baseline traffic exists), or the magic-moment activation rate flagging the hero is over-promising.
